===HINTS===
|just look at those gorgeous ogmos!\n\nogmo isn't a special object. any amount of ogmos can be in a level, and any ogmo can reach a door or a "win" tile to win the level. and if an ogmo dies, the level will only be reset if all other ogmos are dead as well. if you die here, any other ogmo can take your place... how existential!\n\n(also take especial notice to how you can alternate between standing/looking up/ducking without it interrupting your blinking pattern)
|that block up there is a gost block. it copies your movements, and causes the first loaded ogmo to die if it dies. (most levels with it only have one ogmo, so this can be simplified to "you die if it dies".) you can also make contact with it to safely eliminate it from the level.\n\naside from copying ogmo's movement, it can't do most things that ogmos can do, such as win a level, activate hint blocks, or use doors.
