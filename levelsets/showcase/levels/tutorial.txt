===HINTS===
|ogmo (the short guy) can jump, and double-jump. yellow arrows will refill ogmo's double-jump if he's already used it. align yourself next to the block there and jump to the next hint block.
|ogmo (the red guy) can also wall-jump. jump into the wall to the right and don't let go of the "right" key. when you're close to the lower spike-balls, jump, without letting go of "right". you'll jump off of the wall. this will come in handy in actual levels, since it doesn't use up your double-jump.
|ogmo (the jumpy guy) can do an extra-high jump known as a "skid jump". jump out of this crawlspace, and keep moving right. when you get near the wall, start moving left and immediately jump. try to reach the next hint block without wall-jumping.
|and that's how ogmo (the cool guy) navigates through levels.\n\npress R to return to the start of the level - or get back on your own, if you're feeling up to it.
